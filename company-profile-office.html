Positive Vibes
Office Life
An Awesome Atmosphere
According to co-workers, Taboola makes every effort to keep its employees in a healthy, happy, and creative atmosphere throughout their workdays. Yoga balls can be found all around the office, walking desks—or treadmill desks—are accessible for energized work sessions, and a grand piano is even available for people to enjoy playing throughout the day.

Office Culture
Team Time
From the company kitchen to ping-pong tables in the office game room to Friday afternoon beers to company-wide happy hours, Taboola employees are offered ample opportunities to enjoy each other's company. The office also hosts a speaker series and Taboola University—weeklong training for new hires—for co-workers to come together under educational platforms as well.
