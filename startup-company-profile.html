What Banfield Pet Hospital Does
Banfield 101
Founded as a veterinary practice in 1955, Banfield Pet Hospital, a subsidiary of the Mars corporation, has grown to nearly 925 hospitals in 42 states, as well as the District of Columbia and Puerto Rico. With more than 2,600 veterinarians, Banfield oversees around 8 million pet visits a year, guided by the five principles—quality, responsibility, mutuality, efficiency, and freedom—to provide high‐quality healthcare for each pet.
